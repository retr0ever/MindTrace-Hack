MindTrace — EEG Cleaning Web App

Upload a raw EEG dataset (.npy or .csv). MindTrace removes common noise (e.g. eye blinks and electrical hum) and prepares an easy-to-read explanation plus an ElevenLabs audio file.

What this app does

  • Checks your EEG signal for obvious problems (missing or invalid values).
  • Removes slow drifts and high-frequency noise with standard filters.
  • Reduces large spikes such as eye blinks so the brain activity is clearer.
  • Produces a cleaned signal you can download and a short, human-readable summary.
